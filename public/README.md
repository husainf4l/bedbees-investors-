# Hero Image Placeholder

This folder should contain your hero image for the website.

## Required Image:

**Filename:** `hero.jpg`

**Recommended Specifications:**
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080px (minimum)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Subject:** Travel-related imagery such as:
  - World map or globe
  - Airplane in flight
  - Beautiful natural landscapes
  - Global city skylines
  - Travel destinations
  - People traveling/exploring

**Where to find images:**
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/travel)
- [Pexels](https://pexels.com/search/travel/)
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/images/search/travel/)

## How to add your image:

1. Download or create your hero image
2. Rename it to `hero.jpg`
3. Place it in the `/public` folder
4. The website will automatically use it

## Temporary Placeholder:

Until you add your own image, you can use any free stock photo from the sources above. The image should evoke a sense of global travel, adventure, and connection.
